Ashok Kumar Ratan

I am a 3rd-year BA.LL.B student at Assam University with a deep interest in the intersection of law, technology, and public policy. Through JurisVista, I aim to simplify complex legal topics, publish insightful articles, and provide a platform for law students to share their research and perspectives.

My experiences span legal research, drafting, and litigation assistance in internships with firms, courts, and policy-focused organizations. I’ve participated in moot courts, published articles on cyber laws, disability rights, and constitutional issues, and earned certifications including the SEBI Investor Awareness Exam and a Legal Drafting Course.

I believe law should be accessible, understandable, and evolving — and JurisVista is my step in that direction.
